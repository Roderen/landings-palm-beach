THE PALM BEACH TOWER 3
The Palm Beach Tower 3 is the most recent project by Nakheel, offering 1–3 bedroom apartments in one of the most desirable areas of Dubai, with access to a private beach.

The Palm Beach Tower 3 is part of the larger Palm Beach Towers project, which consists of three towers, including a service building, commercial tower with offices, and a residential tower, which will be located on a mid-rise podium, where residential units will be located from the 10th to 51st floors without penthouses.

The project is developed by Nakheel, who is one of the leading Emirati developers, and is known for projects on Palm Jumeirah, The World, Jumeirah Village, Al Furjan, The Gardens, International City and many more.

ENTERTAINMENT ACTIVITIES
A wide range of entertainment and leisure options, located just a 5-15 minute drive away, will be available to residents of The Palm Beach Tower 3, including the following options:

Dubai Marina Mall is one of the most popular shopping destinations among locals and tourists, offering over 140 shops, 21 dining options and entertainment activities.

Nakheel Mall is a luxury shopping mall, located in the center of Palm Jumeirah. Apart from 140 retail stores and 30 dining options, visitors can also find VOX Cinemas, Trampo Extreme and Fabyland.

Aquaventure Waterpark is the best place to experience rides and attractions. Among the most popular rides are Zoomerango, Shockwave and Leap of Faith.

Ain Dubai is the largest observation wheel in the world, offering amazing views of Dubai and the Arabian Gulf. It's the perfect place to celebrate birthdays and other events.

The Lost Chambers Aquarium will be appreciated by people of any age, as it's home to 65,000 sea creatures, including sharks, rays, jellyfish, piranhas and much more.

The Walk at JBR is a waterfront promenade, offering many retail and dining options.

The Beach at JBR is one of the best places in Dubai to relax along the shore of the Arabian Gulf, as well as visit The Pavilion with various dining options.
